Features

Clinic Queue Manager offers everything your clinic needs for efficient, stress-free patient flow.

  • Digital Queue Registration: Patients register and get a queue number instantly.
  • Staff Dashboard: Easy-to-use dashboard for managing patient flow.
  • SMS/Email Notifications: Patients receive alerts when their turn is near.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Track queue times, patient volume, and more.
  • Mobile-Friendly: Works on any device for staff and patients.

How It Works

  1. Patient registers and receives a queue number.
  2. Staff manage the queue via dashboard.
  3. Patient gets notified when their turn is near.

About Us

Empowering clinics and hospitals to deliver better patient experiences through smart queue management.

Our Story

Clinic Queue Manager was founded after witnessing the frustration and inefficiency caused by long, unmanaged queues in Garissa hospitals. Our team saw patients waiting for hours, staff overwhelmed, and clinics struggling to keep order. We set out to build a solution that would transform the way healthcare facilities operate, making visits smoother for everyone.

Problems We've Solved

  • Long Waiting Times: Reduced average patient wait by 50% in pilot clinics.
  • Overcrowded Waiting Rooms: Digital queue numbers and notifications keep spaces clear and safe.
  • Staff Overload: Automated dashboard helps staff manage patient flow with ease.
  • Poor Communication: SMS and email alerts keep patients informed and reduce anxiety.
